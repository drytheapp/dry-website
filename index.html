Good questions

Everything you need to know.

Is there a fee to use Dry?
A 5% booking fee applies to every order — shown at checkout before you confirm. Dry cleaners pay a flat monthly subscription and keep 100% of their order revenue.
What is Quick Drop?
After a few orders at a location, Quick Drop unlocks automatically. Drop your bag with just a QR code — staff logs every item and sends you an itemized estimate to approve before anything is charged.
How does payment work?
Add a card once in your account. Standard orders are charged at confirmation. Quick Drop orders are charged only after you approve the itemized estimate from the cleaner.
What is hang dry?
An add-on for Launder & Press and Wash & Fold orders. Air dry only — no heat, no dryer. Recommended for wool, cashmere, silk, and delicate knits. +$3 per item.
Can I document stains before drop-off?
Yes — photograph any stains or damage during the order flow. Photos go directly to the cleaner so they know exactly what to treat before touching your garment.
How do I get support?
Use the contact form below or tap Help & Support inside the app under your Profile tab. We typically reply within a few hours.
Can I save my laundry preferences?
Yes — set preferences like always hang dry knitwear, no fabric softener, or cold wash, and they apply to every order automatically. Find them under Profile in the app.
Are the chemicals safe?
It depends on the cleaner. Some use non-toxic GreenEarth solvents. Every profile states exactly what they use. Filter for non-toxic when you search.

Common questions

Before you sign up.

Do you take a cut of my orders?
No. You keep 100% of what customers pay you for services. Dry. charges customers a separate 5% booking fee on their end. You pay only the flat monthly subscription.
How quickly can I go live?
Immediately. When you sign up, you receive a login link by email. After completing the 4-step onboarding flow in your dashboard, your listing is live and customers can book you.
Can I set my own prices?
Yes, entirely. You set your own per-item and per-service pricing in your dashboard. You can update pricing at any time and changes reflect in the app immediately.
What if I want to cancel?
Cancel anytime from your dashboard settings. You retain access through the end of your paid period. No cancellation fees, no partial refunds, no penalties.
What's the difference between Basic and Pro?
Basic gives you everything you need to run daily operations. Pro adds priority placement in consumer search results, advanced analytics, zip code reports, and at-risk customer alerts.
Do I need any hardware or equipment?
No hardware required. The business dashboard runs in any web browser on any device — tablet, laptop, or desktop. You can manage everything from an iPad at the counter.
