Good questions

Everything you need to know.

Is there a fee to use Dry?
A 5% booking fee applies to every order — shown at checkout before you confirm. It covers digital booking, live tracking, and automatic payment. Dry cleaners pay a flat monthly subscription and keep 100% of their order revenue.
What is Quick Drop?
After a few orders at a location, Quick Drop unlocks automatically. Drop your bag with just a QR code — no form to fill out. Staff logs every item and sends you an itemized estimate to approve before anything is charged.
How does payment work?
Add a card once in your account. Standard orders are charged at confirmation. Quick Drop orders are charged only after you approve the itemized estimate from the cleaner.
What is hang dry?
An add-on for Launder & Press and Wash & Fold orders. Air dry only — no heat, no dryer. Recommended for wool, cashmere, silk, and delicate knits. Toggle it on when you assign garments. +$3 per item.
Can I document stains before drop-off?
Yes — photograph any stains or damage during the order flow. Photos go directly to the cleaner so they know exactly what to treat before touching your garment.
Are the chemicals safe?
It depends on the cleaner. Some use non-toxic GreenEarth liquid silicone solvents — no harsh chemicals, no odor. Every profile states exactly what they use. Filter for non-toxic when you search.
Can I save my laundry preferences?
Yes — set preferences like always hang dry knitwear, no fabric softener, cold wash, or separate darks, and they apply to every order automatically. Find them under Profile → Laundry Preferences in the app.
How do I get support?
Use the contact form below, or tap Help & Support inside the app under your Profile tab. We typically reply within a few hours.

Common questions

Before you apply.

Do you take a cut of my orders?
No. You keep 100% of what customers pay you for services. Dry. charges customers a separate 5% booking fee on their end. You pay only the flat monthly subscription.
How quickly can I go live?
We review applications within one business day. Once approved, your dashboard login is created and your listing goes live in the app immediately.
Can I set my own prices?
Yes, entirely. You set your own per-item and per-service pricing directly in your dashboard. You can update pricing at any time and changes reflect in the app immediately.
What if I want to cancel?
Cancel anytime from your dashboard. You retain access through the end of your paid period. No cancellation fees, no partial refunds, no penalties.
What's the difference between Basic and Pro?
Basic gives you everything you need to run daily operations — orders, notifications, analytics, and your listing. Pro adds priority placement in consumer search results, advanced analytics, zip code reports, and at-risk customer alerts.
Do I need any hardware or equipment?
No hardware required. The business dashboard runs in any web browser on any device — tablet, laptop, or desktop. You can manage everything from an iPad at the counter.
